Me and Victoria

This me and my girlfriend at the annual "barn bash" dance held by one of our student orgs. Each year we dress up as farmers and square dance while doing other farm related activities, like bonfires. We're getting married next year!

Buried in Ocean City

This me buried up to my neck in Ocean City, New Jersey. I spent the summer of 2019 here at a leadership development/mission expedition with over sixty other Cru students from around the country.

Paintaball Match

This is me in my typical paintball gear: mask, goggles, and marker (gun). Back when my cousin and I used to play at this arena, we were called the "bash brothers" because we have never lost a game when placed on the same team.

My Dog Jazmine

This is my second dog, Jazmine. She is 6 years old and currently lives in my house at Northville. She is the greatest dog ever, and if you disagree please feel free to fill out my contact form and educate me about how wrong I am.

I Found a Monkey!

This is me in Nevis, an island in the Carribean known for its ambient primate population. I was eating pretzels one day and this little guy came up to me and crawled on my shoulder. I did not feed him, I merely let him take my food.

Gun Range

This is me at my favorite gun range in Island Lake, Michigan. I am shooting an old WWI infantry rifle given to my grandfather by one of his squadmates as a gift for saving his life in the battle of Okinawa. I love antique firearms, and will always prefer them to modern ones.

My Family

This is me and my family, minus my mom who took the picture. My father, younger sister Sophie older sister Emily, and brother-in-law Stephen. We celebrating my dad's 55th birthday.

Batting Practice with the Tigers

This is me and my best friend Ryan at Comerica Park. Back in highschool, we played a lot of baseball together, and still hit batting practice from time to time. One day, my dad surprised us with an invitation to hit pregame batting practice with our pro team. In the center is Joel Zumaya, a retired closing pitcher we liked from when we were kids.

Cuban Tobacco Farmer

This is me at a tobacco plantation in Vinalez, Cuba. My team and I went here after a week of installing water filters in poorer communities for the previous week. While there, I had an awesome conversation with a farmer named Miguel Cortez, who had one of the wisest outlooks on life I have ever witnessed.
